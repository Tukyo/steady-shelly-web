January 1, 2025: Launch

Official launch of Steady Shelly, marking the beginning of our journey.

Q1 2025: RedPill Phase

Initial phase focused on learning and adaptation. Both Shelly and the development team will be in an intensive learning period, gathering insights and refining strategies.

Q2-Q3 2025: Expand Shelly's Functionality

Integration of various APIs and Telegram for external long-term data storage. This will allow users to view the data Shelly is working with, including top wallet lists, current trades, and past trades. The goal is to make all relevant information available and transparent.

Q4 2025: Evaluation and Planning

Assessment of Shelly's performance and the project's financial stability. If successful, this phase will lay the groundwork for future expansions.

2026 and Beyond: ShellyDAO and ShellyTrade Terminal

Contingent on Shelly's success and the project's financial stability, we aim to launch ShellyDAO and the ShellyTrade Terminal. These will be advanced platforms leveraging the data and insights gathered throughout Shelly's operation.
